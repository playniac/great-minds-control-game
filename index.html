Tetris

Keyboard Controls

← / → Move piece left/right

↓ Soft drop (faster fall)

Z Rotate counter-clockwise

X Rotate clockwise

Space Hard drop (instant)

P Pause/Resume

Mouse/Touch Controls

Left Click Move left

Right Click Move right

Down Drag Soft drop

Left Drag Rotate counter-clockwise

Right Drag Rotate clockwise

Center Drag Up Hard drop

Game Goal

Clear horizontal lines by filling them completely. Each line cleared scores points and increases your level.
